Clinical features

  • Symptoms
    1. Chronic irritation
    2. Itching
    3. Mild lacrimation
    4. Gluing of cilia
    5. Mild photophobia
  • Signs
    1. Yellow crust
    2. Small ulcers
    3. Red, thickened lid margins
    4. Mild papillary conjunctivitis and conjunctival hyperemia
    5. Lash abnormalities
    6. Tylosis
    7. Recurrent styes
    8. Eversion of punctum
